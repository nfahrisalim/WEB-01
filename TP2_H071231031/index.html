Kamen rider Gavv

Snacks and Evil, I'll eat them up!!!!

Kamen Rider Gavv (仮面ライダーガヴ, Kamen Raidā Gavu) is a Japanese tokusatsu drama in Toei Company's Kamen Rider Series. It is the sixth series to debut in the Reiwa Era and the thirty-ninth overall.

About

The looming threat of monsters. Granutes, monsters from another world. Humans have no idea they are secretly coming to their world. Humans have no idea their kind are being hunted.

One young man steps up to protect humanity. Shoma is a young man who loves to eat. By eating sweets, he can create beings known as the Gochizo that become his companions and his main source of power as Kamen Rider Gavv. The story of Shoma, who fights to protect people's happiness from the Granute, begins now!
